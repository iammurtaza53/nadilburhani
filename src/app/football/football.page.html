FOOTBALL ACADEMY

Football at Al Nadi-al-Burhani

The court size is 40 m × 20 m (44 yd × 22 yd). The inner edges of the vertical goal posts is 3 m (3.3 yd) apart, and the lower edge of the horizontal crossbar supported by the goal posts is 2 m (2.2 yd) above the ground. Nets made of hemp, jute or nylon are attached to the back of the goalposts and crossbar. The depth of the goal is 80 cm (31 in) at the top and 1 m (3.3 ft) at the bottom.

Some tips to avoid injuries when playing any Sports

  • Drink water before, during and after play.
  • Wear broad-spectrum sunscreen, sunglasses and a hat, even in cloudy conditions.
  • Always warm up, stretch and cool down.
  • Good technique and practice will help prevent injury.
  • Wear the right protective equipment.
  • Seek professional advice on footwear.
